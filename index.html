Kapil Sharma

About Kapil

Kapil Sharma is an Indian stand-up comedian, television presenter, actor and producer known for hosting The Kapil Sharma Show. His impeccable comic timing and witty humor have made him one of India's most loved entertainers.

Highlights

  • Winner of The Great Indian Laughter Challenge
  • Host of Comedy Nights with Kapil
  • Starred in films like Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon
  • Known for his live comedy shows
